About Me

I’m a 23 years old Business Informatics student at Széchenyi István University in Győr, set to graduate this summer.
Currently, I live in Győr, Hungary, and work full-time as a junior Laravel full-stack developer at Genion Group Zrt.

Throughout my career, I've worked on multiple projects explained in the projects section including CRM systems, web applications, and Unity-based games. This experience has fueled my passion for building useful and efficient systems.

I’m always eager to explore new technologies, methods, and creative ideas. While my primary focus is full-stack development, I’m also deeply interested in machine learning and AI models.
